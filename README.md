**Visa Job Intel Pro** is a high-performance global job search engine designed to solve the "Sponsorship Black Hole." Unlike standard job boards, this tool uses **Decision Intelligence** to score every job lead based on its likelihood of providing visa sponsorship (UK, USA, Canada, EU) and matches it against your specific career profile.

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 🎯 1. Visa Probability Scoring
Our proprietary engine scans job descriptions for legal keywords (CoS, Tier 2, H1B, LMIA, Blue Card) and locations to assign a **Sponsorship Match Score (0-100%)**. Stop wasting hours reading descriptions that don't apply to international talent.

### 🧬 AI Resume-Job Matcher
Paste your resume directly into the sidebar. The app uses a real-time comparison algorithm to calculate your **Job-Fit Percentage**, allowing you to prioritize roles where you have the highest chance of getting an interview.

### 🕵️ Anti-Ghost Job Technology
Recruitment platforms are often filled with expired or "ghost" listings. Every job card includes a **"Search on Company Site"** button that generates a direct Google query for the employer's official career page, bypassing the middleman and increasing your success rate.

### 💰 Local Intelligence (CoL)
Integrated insights for major global hubs (London, Berlin, Toronto, NYC, Dubai, Amsterdam). The app provides:
- **Estimated Rent Costs**
- **Safety Ratings**
- **Salary Benchmarking** (Know exactly what you need to earn to live comfortably).

### 🤖 40+ Specialized Niches
From **AI & Machine Learning** and **Nursing** to **Skilled Trades** and **Aviation**, the engine uses advanced Boolean Logic to find high-value roles across 40 different industries.
